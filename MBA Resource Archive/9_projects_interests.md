## Panel Insights Worth Saving

**Uber Pricing Manager → VP (Alumni Edge):**
Revenue management and pricing are where the real leverage is at scale. The person who shifts a company from growth to profitability is in RevOps/Pricing.

**Sedhu Krishnamurthy, Microsoft — GM AI Transformation:**
AI transformation in enterprises is about *organization metabolism* — how quickly adoption happens. The bottleneck is change management, not technology.

**Dixie Adams, IBM — M&A Integration:**
Technology ↔ business insight is the rare skill. "AI can excel at repetitive actions." The human edge is in ideation and stakeholder navigation. Journal everything.

**The Trade Desk (Panels):**
First-party data is the moat in advertising. In mature industries, focus on margin growth, not volume. Brand = operational excellence when TAM isn't expanding.

**GATX (Industrials Conference):**
Railroads are effectively unregulated monopolies. Don't reduce price when a competitor moves in — differentiate on service. Long-term focus beats short-term volume chasing.
